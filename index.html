Our Vision

Face Challenges:

The vision of Face It committee is to empower engineering students to face thechallenges they encounter during their academic journey. 

Culture of Innovation:

Face It committee envisions a student community that fosters a culture of innovation. By providing students with opportunities to engage inresearch,explore new ideas and collaborate with their peers. 

Diversity:

The vision of Face It committee is to promote inclusivity in the student community. The committee strives to create an environment where students from diverse backgrounds can thrive and feel included.
